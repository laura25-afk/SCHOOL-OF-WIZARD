Your Project. General Information

Welcome to the school of Wizards. Here, you will become an excellent wizard by developing your skills in English and ICT

The aim of this project is to promote the creativity of the students by attending The School of Wizards.  Therefore, it is essential to surf different websites and overcome several tasks that will help you gain knowledge and acquire strategies to be a Great Wizard.

You will have to do different missions along this project and finally you will have to do a big challenge.

It is easy if you do right all the previous missions. Are you ready?

When you finish all  your missions and your last challenge, you will get your Certificate of participation:

You can download the certificate in an editable file or a  pdf file

Along this project:

We are learning...

  • to debate and take decisions
  • to understand  and produce oral and written messages
  • to interact and mediate orally and with written information
  • to explain information
  • to remember details
  • to read awareness
  • to develop research skills and visual design skills
  • to solve  problems and to find solutions
  • to use ICT to  record a  podcast 
  • to use ICT to  create a digital map, a digital poster, a digital scrapbook and a label
  • to search for information on the Internet
  • to learn  vocabulary related to explorer trips  (activities, material, places), to animals’ body parts, aromatic plants,  ICT and  free time activities
  • to express actions in the past with regular and irregular verbs
  • to express strong obligation and necessity
  • to read and follow instructions
  • to explain instructions to their classmates
  • to practice the pronunciation of must and mustn’t
  • to know activities  and trips they could do if they attended an ordinary school
  • to know famous castles and their location
  • to do descriptions using adjectives in comparative and superlative form
  • to know mythical creatures and practice the pronunciation
  • to review pronouns (subject, object, adjective possessive and possessive)
  • to debate, on the one hand, about having pets at home or not and, on the other hand, about buying or rescuing animals to have them as pets.
  • to learn how to take care about animals
  • to express certainty, necessity, obligation and lack of obligation
  • to learn directions to make potions and to practice the pronunciation
  • to express measurements when making a potion
  • to make a cauldron with paper mache,
  • to reflect about the 2030 Agenda  by creating a potion to grant a SDG
  • to express future intentions and plans
  • to learn to how to learn by playing Trivial
  • to express probability or possibility and predictions

How?

By an Active Learning Methodology that makes you active in your learning process during classroom activities. These strategies promote a change of focus in the teaching-learning process. Therefore, we refer to it as a student-centred methodology. Active learning techniques include role-playing, group projects, think-pair-share, peer teaching, debates,  class discussion, etc.

Your teacher

Your teacher will act mainly as:

  • informant and resource, providing suitable input (dialogues, narratives, descriptions, word meanings, etc.);
  • assessor and monitor of meaningful learning experiences;
  • diagnoser of interests and needs;
  • motivator, prompter and generator of positive attitudes;
  • provider, organiser, controller and participant of second language acquisition situation

Your Work

Before doing the final challenge you will have to do 5 MISSIONS. The tasks that you will have done at the end of this project are:

  • 1st Mission: designing a digital map with information to guarantee safety.
